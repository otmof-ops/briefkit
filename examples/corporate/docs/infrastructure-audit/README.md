# IT Infrastructure Audit Report

## Abstract

This report presents the findings of a comprehensive IT infrastructure audit
conducted for ACME Corporation between January and March 2026. The assessment
evaluated cloud migration readiness, security posture, and operational resilience
across ACME's three primary data centers located in Dallas (DC-SOUTH), Chicago
(DC-CENTRAL), and Portland (DC-WEST).

The audit scope encompassed 47 production servers, 12 SaaS applications, network
infrastructure across all three facilities, and supporting operational processes
including disaster recovery, change management, and incident response. Assessment
methodologies aligned to NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0 and ISO 27001:2022
controls, with supplemental evaluation against CIS Benchmarks v8 for host-level
configuration.

The assessment identified 2 critical findings, 4 high-severity findings, 5
medium-severity findings, and 3 low-severity findings. Critical findings include
unpatched public-facing servers with known exploited vulnerabilities and the
absence of multi-factor authentication on administrative accounts. The report
concludes with a prioritized remediation roadmap requiring an estimated investment
of $340,000 over six months, with an expected aggregate risk reduction of 73
percent upon full implementation.
