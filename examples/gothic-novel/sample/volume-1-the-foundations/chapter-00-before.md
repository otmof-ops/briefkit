The opening volume begins before light, before time, before language. The four-thousand-year line from the void to a coffin in Goshen passes through every covenant the texts record.

This sample chapter exists to show you what the typesetter does with various Markdown patterns. Real chapters omit this scaffolding.

The same hand that closed the coffin in Goshen opened the sea at Migdol four hundred years later.
