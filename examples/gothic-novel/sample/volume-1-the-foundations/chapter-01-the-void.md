# Chapter 1: The Void

---

There was nothing. I want you to understand what that means.

Not darkness. Darkness is something. Darkness is the absence of light, which requires light to have existed first, which requires a system capable of producing it.

The void. Wavelength: undefined. Frequency: zero. Photon count: zero. No electromagnetic field, because no field. No curvature, because no manifold. No temperature, because no particles. Only the one who was about to speak.

I was there.

---

Before the light, I made witnesses.

The story of what I made cannot be understood without the story of who was watching me make it. The angels were first.

She said: "How can this be?" And I said: "All things are possible."
