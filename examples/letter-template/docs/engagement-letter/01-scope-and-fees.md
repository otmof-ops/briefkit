# Scope of Services and Fee Estimate

We have been instructed to provide the following estate planning services:

## Scope of Work

1. **Last Will and Testament** — preparation of a comprehensive will including testamentary trusts for minor beneficiaries, specific bequests, and residuary estate distribution
2. **Enduring Power of Attorney** — financial and legal decision-making authority in the event of incapacity
3. **Appointment of Enduring Guardian** — medical and lifestyle decision-making authority
