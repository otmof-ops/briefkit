# The Backrooms

A horror novel set in the labyrinthine spaces that exist between the walls of the known world. When a person no-clips out of reality, they find themselves in the Backrooms — a vast network of empty rooms, humming fluorescent lights, and the persistent smell of damp carpet. Some find their way out. Most do not.

This example demonstrates the BriefKit book template applied to a novel-length fiction manuscript: multiple parts structured as numbered files, a preface, and a glossary of world-specific terminology.
