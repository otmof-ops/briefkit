# Foreword

This workshop manual is intended for the competent owner, enthusiast, and independent workshop technician undertaking a comprehensive engine and drivetrain rebuild. It demonstrates the OEM service-manual pattern for BriefKit, structured as numbered service groups with consistent per-chapter sections.

## How This Manual Is Organized

Content is divided into eight major parts:

- **Part 1 — General Information** (Groups 01 to 06): foreword, usage notes, abbreviations, safety, technical data, special tools
- **Part 2 — Engine Mechanical** (Groups 10 to 19): block, head, lubrication, cooling
- **Part 3 — Engine Management** (Groups 20 to 28): fuel, turbo, injection, exhaust, ignition
- **Part 4 — Drivetrain** (Groups 30 to 40): clutch, gearbox, axles
- **Part 5 — Removal and Installation** (Groups 50 to 55): engine drop, timing belt
- **Part 6 — Configuration Options** (Groups 70 to 75): variant paths, ECU coding
- **Part 7 — Build Execution** (Groups 80 to 84): build sequence, commissioning, maintenance
- **Part 8 — Technical Appendices** (Groups 90 to 98): torque data, part numbers, index
